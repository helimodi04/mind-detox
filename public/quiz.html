Mind Awareness Quiz

This is a gentle check-in to see how you're doing. There are no right or wrong answers.

1. When you’re alone, what do you think about?

2. Do you overthink?

3. Do you keep feelings inside?

4. How do you know you’re upset?

5. Do you feel lonely even around people?

6. What do you do first when stressed?

7. What small things calm you down?

8. How do you feel when you think about the future?

9. How do you talk to yourself when things go wrong?

10. If you could change one thing about how you feel every day, what would it be?
